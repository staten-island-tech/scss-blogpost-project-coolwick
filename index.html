I Watch People Eat

Do you watch mukbangs? It was originated in Japan meaning "eating show", and you just literally watch people eat. Some people find it weird like ASMR and stuff but I kind of enjoy it. I think they made it because in Japan theres this many people are workaholics and kind of lonely, so during meal times they watch videos of other people eating to simulate eating with someone. These videos would pop up on my YouTube feed and the next thing you know I watched someone eat for 15 minutes. The Internet really is a wonderful place.
